Red Dead Redemption 2

Review

This game is a masterpiece. Although I used to ignore it because most people said it was slow-paced and lacked gunplay, I was wrong to do so. The game is indeed slow-paced but beautifully crafted. It starts slow with character introductions and basic mechanics, but the pace picks up after a few hours of gameplay. The environment, charactersâ€™ personalities and voices, and quests are all spot-on and perfectly implemented in the game. Even the horses are loyal companions that build an innocent and special bond with Arthur, despite him being a bad person in the story. The way they respond to our whistle and get frightened when seeing a predator, and Arthur calming them down are small things that impact the player.
